# Coursework Disclaimer

This repository is based on a group coursework project completed during the MSc Business Analytics: Operational Research and Risk Analysis programme at the University of Manchester.

The repository is intended as a portfolio case study. It highlights selected analytical methods, code structure and business interpretation.

My contribution focused on MCDA, DEA, MOLP analysis, Python implementation with AI-assisted coding support, and interpretation of results for business recommendations.

No confidential or restricted university material is intentionally shared beyond the anonymised case-study data provided for the assignment.
